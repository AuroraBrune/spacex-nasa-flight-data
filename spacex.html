  1. Hopes for Mars
    June 10, 2001

    Millionaire, Elon Musk, wanted to put an experimental greenhouse on the planet Mars. He was interested in increasing public interest in space travel and improve funding to NASA. He made attempts to purchase three ICBMs from Russia.

  2. SpaceX Founded

    Elon Musk realized he could start a company that could open space travel to civilians.

  3. NASA $278 Million Award

The Origins of SpaceX
Headquarters are in Hawthorne, California. SpaceX was formed by entrepreneur Elon Musk in the hopes of revolutionizing the aerospace industry and making affordable spaceflight a reality. The company entered the arena with the Falcon 1 rocket, a two-stage liquid-fueled craft designed to send small satellites into orbit.

“You want to wake up in the morning and think the future is going to be great - and that’s what being a spacefaring civilization is all about. It’s about believing in the future and thinking that the future will be better than the past. And I can’t think of anything more exciting than going out there and being among the stars.” -Elon Musk
